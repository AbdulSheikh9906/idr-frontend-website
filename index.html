About the Institute of Digital Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led institute uniquely positioned at the intersection of academia and real-world industry application. We focus on the most critical areas of the modern digital landscape: digital risk, cybersecurity, AI governance, and technology risk management.

By bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical deployment, we prepare professionals and organizations to navigate and secure high-consequence, regulated environments. Our work with universities and industry partners ensures that our training and frameworks are at the forefront of the field.
